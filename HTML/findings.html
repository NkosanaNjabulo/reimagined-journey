Recommendations for Instant Messaaging Users

Introduction

For Mental Health:

In the rare cases where Instant Messaging affects a user negatively recommendations can be made. It is advisable for a user whose mental health is negatively affected by instant messaging to restrict the time they spend on IM services and similar apps on their phones. It would also be advisable for the to turn off the notifications of the IM services they use, as this limits the number of times a user checks their phone. Lastly, if possible, a digital detox would serve as a better solution. It entails disconnecting from all electronic devices and spend time engaging in offline activities that bring you joy and relaxation.

For Work-Life Balance:

To those struggling to maintain a healthy work-life balance, setting clear work hours and disengaging completely is recommendable. Alternatively, users could separate workspaces and utilise separate devices for their work and personal lives. This allows them to decompress and only participate in work activities when it is appropriate to do so.

For Sociability:

To improve sociability, initiating conversations is effective as it increases the frequency of conversations had by over 20%. The technique promotes social skills and strengthens friendships
overall. Another strategy used to increase sociability is to use voice notes and video messages as
using these features can make your conversations more personal and dynamic.
